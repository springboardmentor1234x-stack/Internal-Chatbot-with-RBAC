welcome and introduction

company vision and mission
at finsolve technologies our vision is to empower businesses and individuals through innovative technology solutions. our mission is to deliver high quality sustainable products and services that create value for our stakeholders.

core values
integrity we act with honesty and transparency.
respect we value diversity and treat everyone with dignity.
innovation we encourage creativity and continuous improvement.
customer focus our customers are at the heart of everything we do.
accountability we take responsibility for our actions and results.

company overview
founded in 2016 finsolve technologies is a leading player in fintech with a presence across india and global markets. we are committed to ethical business social responsibility and fostering a culture of learning and growth.

employee benefits

statutory benefits
employees provident fund epf 12 percent employer and employee contribution as per epf act 1952.
employee state insurance esi for employees earning up to rs 21000 per month covers medical disability maternity.
gratuity payable after 5 years of continuous service as per payment of gratuity act.
maternity benefit 26 weeks paid leave for first two children as per maternity benefit act.
bonus as per payment of bonus act minimum 8.33 percent of basic salary.

company benefits
group health insurance family floater policy covering employee spouse and up to 2 children.
accident and life insurance additional coverage for accidental death or disability.
wellness programs annual health checkups yoga meditation sessions mental health counseling.
employee assistance program confidential counseling and support for personal or professional issues.
flexible work arrangements remote work flexible hours role dependent.

leave policies

types of leave
privilege or annual leave 15 to 21 days per year as per state act accrued monthly.
sick leave 12 days per year non cumulative medical certificate required for more than 2 days.
casual leave 7 days per year state specific.
maternity leave 26 weeks for first two children 12 weeks for subsequent children.
paternity leave 7 to 15 days as per company policy.
bereavement leave 3 to 7 days for immediate family.
compensatory off for work on holidays or weekends with manager approval.
public holidays 10 to 14 days per year as per company calendar and state notifications.
leave without pay allowed with approval after exhausting paid leave.

leave application process
apply via hrms at least 3 days in advance except emergencies.
sick leave for more than 2 days requires medical certificate.
emergency leave must be informed immediately.
approval from manager and hr required.
leave balance visible in hrms.

public holidays policy
annual holiday list circulated at the start of each year.
includes national festival and restricted holidays.
employees may choose restricted holidays.
